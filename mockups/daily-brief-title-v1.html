Design notes
Title treatment. Space Grotesk 700 on both surfaces, matching the broader Vela type system. Home card uses 16px (fits within a card); full-page header uses 24px. Title is sentence case ("Daily brief"), not title case ("Daily Brief").
Date treatment. Inter 500 muted gray, 12–13px. Secondary information. Full weekday + month + day format ("Friday, April 18, 2026") so users have clear context without scrolling.
Sentence vs title case. Vela's broader voice leans toward sentence case. "Daily brief" not "Daily Brief". Recommend applying this change consistently across both surfaces.
Naming inconsistency flagged (separate decision)
"Digest" vs "Brief". The home card currently labels the content "Daily digest"; the full page title is "Daily Brief". Sarah called this out on the call ("if you say digest there, then it should be a digest here and not a brief here"). You told me not to change copy for now, so v1 keeps "Daily brief" everywhere in the wireframe on the assumption we standardise to that.
