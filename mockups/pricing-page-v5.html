What changed in v5
v5 addresses four things: signals are now named explicitly as the core perk, the redundant "Billing cycle" label is gone, delta chips removed now that the visual hierarchy carries the upsell, and vertical space tightened to push Premium higher on mobile.
Signals named, not assumed. Standard's headline feature is now "Buy, sell, and wait signals on 8 assets" instead of "Up to 8 tracked assets". States the actual product (signals) instead of a quantity (assets). Premium's differentiator reads "Signals on every asset Vela covers" so the upgrade is framed as signal coverage, not asset-count trivia. Doesn't rely on the splash to have done this work.
"Billing cycle" label removed. The tabs are self-explanatory. "Billed monthly / annually" line under each price still lives on the card so the billing consequence is legible there. Removes one vertical block above the plans, helping Premium appear closer to the fold.
Delta chips gone. With the green tint, deeper shadow, thicker border, signal-green badge, and "Everything in Standard, plus:" header, the Premium upsell is already obvious. The chips were doing redundant work and adding visual noise.
Features-to-CTA spacing tightened. Margin under the features list reduced from 18px to 12px. Last-row padding-bottom trimmed. Small change, but compounds across both cards.
